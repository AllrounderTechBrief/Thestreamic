About

Independent, vendor‑neutral and designed for media engineers.

Our Platform

Streamic is a specialized technology-news aggregator that curates the latest updates from trusted global publishers. We focus strictly on the engineering side of media: signal flow, Master Control, production IT, and cloud virtualization.

We provide proper attribution for every story, linking directly to the original creators to ensure the broadcast community supports the journalists and publishers who report on our industry.

Technical Independence

We are a self-funded project. We don't write the news; we refine the noise, providing a high-speed, automated editorial hub for the architects who build the modern media world.

Founder

I’m Prerak Mehta. With a background in production IT and broadcast operations, I developed Streamic to bridge the gap between fragmented news feeds and the need for a unified, clean source of engineering intelligence.
