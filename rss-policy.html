RSS Policy

Last Updated: February 6, 2026

1. RSS Aggregation

The Streamic aggregates content from publicly available RSS feeds provided by broadcast technology publishers and news sources.

2. How We Use RSS Feeds

We use RSS feeds to:

  • Collect publicly available headlines and links
  • Display brief previews of articles
  • Provide attribution to original sources
  • Direct users to full content on publisher websites

3. Content Ownership

All content accessed via RSS feeds remains the property of the original publisher. We do not:

  • Reproduce complete articles
  • Claim ownership of aggregated content
  • Modify or alter original headlines or excerpts
  • Republish content beyond brief previews

4. Linking Policy

Every headline links directly to the original article on the publisher's website, ensuring:

  • Traffic is directed to the source
  • Users access full, unmodified content
  • Publishers receive proper attribution and engagement

5. Feed Sources

We only aggregate from:

  • Publicly available RSS feeds
  • Reputable broadcast technology publishers
  • Industry-recognized news sources

6. Publisher Requests

If you are a publisher and wish to:

  • Have your feed removed from our aggregation
  • Modify how your content appears
  • Report an issue with feed handling

Please contact us at: itabmum@gmail.com

7. Compliance

Our RSS aggregation practices comply with standard RSS syndication protocols and respect publisher rights.
